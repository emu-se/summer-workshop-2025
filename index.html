Welcome!

Join us for the first-ever Code with Blocks Summer Workshop, a one-day hands-on coding event designed specifically for high school students with ADHD or on the autism spectrum.

Location: Computer Lab, Pray Harrold Hall, Eastern Michigan University

Date: Wednesday, August 20, 2025

About the Program

This inclusive workshop introduces students to beginner-friendly coding using Scratch, a fun and visual programming platform.

  • Work with Dr. Siyuan Jiang, Computer Science Professor at EMU
  • Follow a guided tutorial to create your own version of the Chrome Dinosaur Game, from the materials developed by Dr. Andrew Begel at Carnegie Mellon University.
  • Plus, an opportunity to create a second creative project: Build your own Virtual Pet! (Tutorials specially developed for this summer workshop.)
  • Lunch and snacks will be provided in the event.

All activities are designed to be structured, supportive, and sensory-considerate.
